Key Services

Four capabilities.
One seamless workflow.

Every service below connects directly to the others. Data captured in scanning feeds directly into modelling. Models feed into simulation. The result is a single, consistent source of truth.

3D Laser Scanning & Point Cloud
Imagine photographing every surface of a facility with a beam of light that records millions of measurements per second, accurate to within 2mm. That is what laser scanning does. The output — a “point cloud” — is an exact digital replica of physical reality.
Why it matters for you: No more working from outdated paper drawings or guessing what is hidden behind a wall. Every revamping or expansion project starts from verified, as-is geometry — eliminating costly rework caused by dimensional surprises.
SmartPlant 3D & Intelligent Modelling
SmartPlant 3D is not just a drawing tool — it is a database. Every object in the model (a pump, a nozzle, a cable tray) carries its engineering data: material, specification, discipline, tag number. When something changes, everything connected to it updates automatically.
Why it matters for you: Drastically fewer manual errors, faster document generation, and a single consistent dataset shared across all engineering disciplines — process, civil, mechanical, electrical and instrumentation all working from the same model.
Digital Twin & Simulation
A Digital Twin is a living, connected model of your plant that mirrors real-world conditions. It allows engineers to simulate scenarios — a process change, a safety event, a new unit integration — before any physical intervention takes place.
Why it matters for you: Test a plant modification virtually before spending a single euro on materials or shutdown time. Validate safety scenarios without risk. Train operators on a digital replica. The digital twin pays for itself many times over.
BIM Integration & As-Built Documentation
BIM (Building Information Modelling) extends 3D intelligence to the full asset lifecycle. PROECO delivers as-built documentation that is fully coordinated across all disciplines and formats — ready for operations, maintenance, and future engineering interventions.
Why it matters for you: Your plant documentation is not a cost — it is an asset. Accurate as-built records reduce shutdown duration, accelerate maintenance decisions and provide the foundation for any future expansion or revamping work.
