Engineering Disciplines

Every discipline.
One team.

Each discipline operates as a specialist unit while sharing a common platform — ensuring seamless data flow, consistent documentation standards and zero information silos across the project.

Process Engineering
Designing, managing and optimising chemical, physical and biological processes using advanced simulation software. Every plant is engineered to comply with client requirements, operational conditions and regulatory safety standards.
  • Mass and energy balance analysis
  • PFD & P&ID development
  • Control and safety valve identification
  • Line specification & piping class definition
  • HAZOP facilitation & process simulation
Civil & Structural Engineering
Metal and reinforced concrete structures within complex industrial plants. Finite element analysis specialists performing stress analysis on static and dynamic events impossible to evaluate with simplified tools.
  • Structural and stress/strain analysis
  • Foundation load capacity assessment
  • Centre of gravity & lifting patterns
  • Primary and secondary metal structure drawings
  • Stairs, landings, gangways, balustrades
Mechanical & Piping Engineering
The critical link between process concept and physical plant. We define the functional and typological characteristics of each equipment or system, including position and all mechanical connections.
  • Equipment lists & technical data sheets
  • Pipeline GA, detail & isometric drawings
  • Stress analysis calculations
  • Piping support dimension & positioning
  • Material list specification
Electrical & Instrumentation
One of PROECO's founding disciplines and a core area of expertise. HV, MV and LV systems design, including full plant control and instrumentation from datasheets to final hook-up drawings.
  • Electric load lists & balance calculations
  • Earthing, protection & cable dimensioning
  • Functional and single-wire diagrams
  • Instrument & valve datasheets
  • Junction box wiring & hook-up drawings
Automation & Control Engineering
Every industrial plant relies on automated control systems. PROECO designs these according to process functional criteria, state-of-the-art technology and the required safety standards.
  • DCS / PLC / ESD system architecture
  • Cause-and-effect diagrams
  • I/O lists & loop diagrams
  • Automation and logic diagrams
  • Control system technical specifications
Fire & Safety Engineering
Comprehensive risk assessment and Fire & Gas system engineering for the most complex and hazardous industrial facilities — from FERA through to detailed detection, alarm and suppression system design.
  • FERA & QRA assessment calculations
  • Hazardous area classification
  • Dispersion study calculations
  • Detection, alarm & extinguishing system drawings
  • ATEX documentation & SIL assessment
Telecommunication Engineering
Specialist expertise in marine and industrial communication systems, developed through extensive work on drillships and semi-submersible platforms. Complete engineering from PAGA to satellite navigation.
  • PAGA, PABX & LAN systems
  • CCTV, GMDSS & radio systems
  • V-Sat & Inmarsat satellite systems
  • SASS (Ship Security Alerting System)
  • Marine navigation system design
LCI & Digitalisation
3D model engineering, laser scanning and digital twin capabilities. Complex projects with high technological content, managed by integrated specialist teams using the most advanced modelling platforms available.
  • SmartPlant 3D / AVEVA E3D & PDMS
  • 3D Laser Scanning (±2mm accuracy)
  • Point cloud & as-built 3D modelling
  • Cadworx Plant Professional
  • Digital Twin development
Management & Project Control
Project management, budget control, scheduling and quality assurance across all project phases. A dedicated management layer that ensures discipline coordination, client interface and delivery performance.
  • Project organisation & budget control
  • Time schedule management
  • Quality assurance (ISO 9001:2015)
  • Document control & deliverable tracking
  • Client reporting & interface management
