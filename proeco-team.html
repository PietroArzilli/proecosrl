The Group

Part of
Parlym Group.

Parlym — Energies & Synergies — is an international group operating across the full energy value chain. With offices spanning Europe, Africa and Asia, Parlym brings together engineering companies that share a common philosophy: technical excellence, client focus and a long-term view.

PROECO operates as Parlym's primary engineering entity for the Oil & Gas, Chemical, Power and Environment sectors. The acquisition of TEA Sistemi SpA in 2025 has further strengthened the group's capability in Technical Safety, Flow Assurance and Process Engineering.

Team Philosophy

What we believe
in.

These aren't values written for a brochure. They are the principles that determine how we staff a project, how we challenge a brief and how we decide what 'good enough' means — which, for us, it never does.

Technical Integrity
We do not compromise on engineering quality under schedule or commercial pressure. Our reputation is built on clients being able to rely on our numbers — unconditionally.
Collective Intelligence
The best engineering decisions come from eight disciplines thinking together, not sequentially. We build project teams that argue, align and deliver as one integrated unit.
Permanent Accountability
105 engineers. All permanent. All accountable. We do not rotate teams between clients — we build long-term relationships where continuity is a feature, not a luxury.
Digital by Default
3D modelling, laser scanning and digital twin are not premium add-ons at PROECO — they are how we engineer. The result is fewer errors, faster reviews and zero dimensional surprises on site.
Sustainable Engineering
We design for the long term — environmentally, operationally and commercially. Our energy transition and environment practice is growing because we believe it is where our skills matter most.
Continuous Growth
Since 2001, every strategic move — from founding to TEA Sistemi's acquisition — has been driven by a single ambition: to be more capable next year than we are today.
